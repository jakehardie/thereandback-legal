Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

There and Back ("the App") is a meditation app developed by Jake Hardie ("we", "us", "our"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data the App collects, how it is used, and your rights.

What Data We Collect

The App is designed to collect as little data as possible. We do not require you to create an account or provide any identifying details to use the App.

The App stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • Meditation progress — which days and sessions you have completed, so the App can track your position in the 30-day programme.
  • Preferences — your notification settings (whether reminders are enabled and at what times), your ambience preferences (rain, calm, volume level), and whether you have completed the onboarding screens.
  • Install date — the date you first opened the App, used solely to calculate your current programme day.

How Data Is Stored

The App does not transmit your meditation progress or preferences to our servers. All data listed above is stored locally on your device. Depending on your device settings, this data may be included in your device backups (for example, iCloud Backup), which are managed by Apple in accordance with Apple's Privacy Policy.

Data We Do Not Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit any of the following:

  • Your name, email address, or any personal contact information (unless you contact us directly)
  • Your location or GPS data
  • Device identifiers, advertising identifiers, or fingerprinting data
  • Health or fitness data
  • Financial or payment information (purchases are handled entirely by Apple)

Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate any third-party analytics tools, advertising networks, tracking SDKs, or data processors. The App does not contain any advertisements.

If you choose to purchase the App or any in-app content, that transaction is processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. Apple's handling of your payment information is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy.

Apple Diagnostics

If you have enabled "Share With App Developers" in your iOS settings, Apple may share aggregated diagnostics and usage statistics with us through App Store Connect. We use this information only to improve app stability and do not use it for advertising, tracking, or profiling.

Contacting Us

If you contact us by email, we will process your email address and the contents of your message in order to respond to you and handle any support or legal queries. We will keep this correspondence only as long as necessary for those purposes and will then delete or anonymise it.

Notifications

The App offers optional daily meditation reminders. These are local notifications scheduled on your device. They are not push notifications sent from a server. No data is transmitted when notifications are scheduled or delivered. You can enable or disable notifications at any time in the App's settings or in your device's system settings.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any personal information from children. Because the App collects no personal data from any user through the App itself, no special provisions for children's data are required.

Your Rights

Under the UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR) and the Data Protection Act 2018, you have rights regarding your personal data.

We do not run user accounts and we do not receive your in-app progress data. If you contact us by email, you can request access to, correction of, or deletion of that correspondence.

You can delete all locally stored App data at any time by:

  • Using the "Reset Progress" option in the App's settings, or
  • Deleting the App from your device, which removes all associated data.

Complaints

If you have concerns about how we handle personal information (for example, messages you send us), you can contact us first using the details below. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the UK supervisory authority, the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO).

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
