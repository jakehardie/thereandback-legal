Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

There and Back ("the App") is a meditation app developed by Jake Hardie ("we", "us", "our"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data the App collects, how it is used, and your rights.

What Data We Collect

The App is designed to collect as little data as possible. We do not collect any personal information, and we do not require you to create an account or provide any identifying details.

The App stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • Meditation progress — which days and sessions you have completed, so the App can track your position in the 30-day programme.
  • Preferences — your notification settings (whether reminders are enabled and at what times), your ambience preferences (rain, calm, volume level), and whether you have completed the onboarding screens.
  • Install date — the date you first opened the App, used solely to calculate your current programme day.

How Data Is Stored

All data is stored locally on your device using Apple's standard on-device storage (UserDefaults). No data is transmitted to any server, cloud service, or third party. We have no ability to access, view, or retrieve any data stored on your device.

Data We Do Not Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit any of the following:

  • Your name, email address, or any personal contact information
  • Your location or GPS data
  • Device identifiers, advertising identifiers, or fingerprinting data
  • Usage analytics, crash reports, or behavioural data
  • Health or fitness data
  • Financial or payment information (purchases are handled entirely by Apple)

Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate any third-party analytics tools, advertising networks, tracking SDKs, or data processors. The App does not contain any advertisements.

If you choose to purchase the App or any in-app content, that transaction is processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. Apple's handling of your payment information is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy.

Notifications

The App offers optional daily meditation reminders. These are local notifications scheduled on your device. They are not push notifications sent from a server. No data is transmitted when notifications are scheduled or delivered. You can enable or disable notifications at any time in the App's settings or in your device's system settings.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any personal information from children. Because the App collects no personal data from any user, no special provisions for children's data are required.

Your Rights

Under the UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR) and the Data Protection Act 2018, you have rights regarding your personal data. However, because the App does not collect or store any personal data on our systems, there is no personal data for us to provide, correct, or delete.

You can delete all locally stored App data at any time by:

  • Using the "Reset Progress" option in the App's settings, or
  • Deleting the App from your device, which removes all associated data.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
